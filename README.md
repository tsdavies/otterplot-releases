# OtterPlot

A gentle writing prompt generator that helps spark your next story. Pick a genre, stir the pot, and let Otley the otter inspire your creativity.

## Download

Grab the latest desktop app for your platform from the [Releases](https://github.com/tsdavies/otterplot-releases/releases/latest) page:

- **macOS** — `.dmg` (Apple Silicon)
- **Windows** — `.exe` installer (Windows 10+)
- **Linux** — `.AppImage` or `.deb` (x86_64)

## Use it in your browser

No download needed — OtterPlot is also available as a web app at **[otterplot.snugpublishing.co.uk](https://otterplot.snugpublishing.co.uk)**.

## About

OtterPlot generates creative writing prompts by combining genres, characters, locations, moods, and more into unique story starters. Whether you're a seasoned writer looking for a warm-up or just getting started, OtterPlot gives you a gentle nudge in an unexpected direction.

Made with care by [Snug Publishing](https://snugpublishing.co.uk).
